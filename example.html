Privacy Policy Of

Blood Mate

Your data is as precious as life itself. We are committed to safeguarding it.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as your name, contact details, and blood type when you register for our app.
  • Location information to help you find nearby blood donation centers.
  • Usage data, including your interactions with the app, to improve our services.
  • Health information related to your eligibility for blood donation.
  • Demographic information for statistical purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Connecting blood donors with recipients in need.
  • Providing you with information about blood donation events and opportunities.
  • Improving our app's functionality and user experience.
  • Ensuring compliance with health and safety regulations for blood donation.
  • Conducting research and analysis to enhance our services.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Your Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional emails.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@blooddonationapp.com.
